Bad Dragon Mystic Review

December 2024

The Mystic is a fantasy inspired toy and is shaped like a unicorn horn. The Mystic is made by Bad Dragon one of the oldest and most well known fantasy toy manufactures. The Mystic has less extreme styling than many fantasy toys but does looks quite elegant. It is a great choice for anyone looking for a non-representative toy that still looks fun and incorperates fantasy elements.
