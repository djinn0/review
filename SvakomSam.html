Design

The Svakom Alex Neo 2 Pro is an automated male masturbator that stimulates through suction and vibrations. The Sam is a plastic cylinder with a powerbutton at the base and an opening to a soft sleeve at the other end. The cap of the Sam has a warming element which is the difference between the pro model and the regular version which does not have a heater. The bottom third of the cylinder houses the motor, controls and battery. On the side of the toy is a USB C port for charging. I like that the Sam Uses a standard charger format like USB C rather than a proprietary connector. The power button has battery charge indicator lights. There is an airpump below the sleeve which produces suction, and a vibrating motor that sits next to the sleeve. The Sleeve is removable for cleaning.
