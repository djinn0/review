Introduction

The Mystic is a fantasy inspired toy and is shaped like a unicorn horn. The Mystic is made by Bad Dragon one of the oldest and best known fantasy toy manufactures.

Design

The Bad Dragon Mystic is designed to look like a unicorn horn. It has a rounded tip and gradually increases in size. It has a slightly wider base with an integrated suction cup. The Mystic has a spiraling groove along the length of the toy.
