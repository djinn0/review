Material & Quality

The Tenga SD's soft sleeve is made of Thermoplastic elastomer (TPE) and the outer casing is made of polyethylene plastic (PE). Neither material is ideal for a reusable toy, for durability and sterility reasons, but are well suited for disposable toy. The TPE is well textured and the PE plastic is light weight making the cup easy to hold. The SD is better designed and feels more luxurious than most disposable toys, including some of Tenga's other offerings
