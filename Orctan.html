Material & Quality

The Orctan masturbator is a premium quality toy. The silicone of the rollers is very smooth and soft. The toy is quite heavy, and much larger than I had anticipated. The plastic of the case has a subtle woven pattern reminiscent of carbon fiber. The battery life is excellent and has never run out during a session. The Orctan uses a standard USB C charger, which is one of my favorite feature bucause I can carge the toy with my phone charger rather then hunting through a box of cords for a proprietary charger. The motors are practically silent and powerful. The motors have sensors which limit torque preventing injury.

Durability & Care

The silicone rollers are soft but feel quite durable. The hinge is beefy and unlikely to wear out. Orctan includes instructions on how maintain battery life including fully charging the toy before first use, removing the toy from the charger when it is fully charged and periodically recharging it even if it has not been used for a period. The rollers can be removed from the frame making them easy to clean. After cleaning the rollers I wipe down the frame. The soft silicone of the toy attracts lint so I keep it in the included storage bag.
