Material & Quality

The Tantus Cone Small is made of medical grade silicone. The surface has a matte texture. It comes in two colors, crimson red or onyx black. The silicone is relatively firm, it holds but has a bit of flex.

Durability & Care

Being made of pure silicone the Cone is very durable. The Cone is quite thick so it is unlikely to be damaged in any way during use. After use I clean the Cone with warm water and antibacteral soap then allow it to dry so it is ready for next time.
