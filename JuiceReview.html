Tantus Juice Plug

Design

The Tantus juice is a medium sized plug, 4 inches in length and 1.4 inches in diameter. It has a smooth tapered shape which makes it easy to insert and pull out. The plug has vertical ribs, they don’t provide any additional sensation on insertion but they can definitely be felt if the plug is twisted. The vertical ribs also hold lube. The neck is large enough to provide a comfortable stretching sensation after insertion but not so large that the plug becomes uncomfortable. The Juice has an anchor shaped base that sits comfortably between the cheeks.

Materials and Qualities

The Juice is made with Tantus’ high quality silicone blend. It has a matte finish which is slick when lubed but doesn’t attract lint. The silicone is firm enough that it doesn’t bend much when the plug in inserted but the plug is a little squishy to the touch. The neck and anchor base feels more pliable than the bulb. The silicone doesn't retain smells after use. The silicone is durable, I have had the Tantus Juice Plug for over a year and it does not show any sign of wear.

Packaging

The Juice, like most Tantus products, came in a clear plastic box. The packaging is simple but effective, it highlights the plug and can be disposed of discreetly. The Juice costs $31 on Tantus’ website which is a pretty reasonable price for a quality plug.
