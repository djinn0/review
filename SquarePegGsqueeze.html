Durability & Care

Being made of pure silicone the G Squeeze is very durable. There are no small details that could be damaged or torn. I expect the toy will last for years of normal use. After use I wash and air dry the G squeeze. It does have a smooth shiny, slightly sticky surface so between uses I store it in a microfiber pouch to keep it from attracting lint. Squarepeg notes that their toys can be sterilized by boiling or by soaking them for 10 min in a 10% bleach solution. A simple wash is sufficient for our needs so I have not tried either sterilization method.

Packaging & Price

The G squeeze was shipped in an unmarked cardboard box and packaged in a branded plastic bag.
