Packaging & Price

The Lucy shipped in an discreet cardboard box. Inside the box the toy was packaged in a simple heat sealed plastic bag wrapped in a branded cotton storage bag. The packaging is similar to other small you brands I have purchased from in the past. Although the packaging is simple, I would much rather have a storage bag then a fancy box.
I purchased my Lucy during the Annual Weredog birthday sale on February 1st for a 25% off discounted. The Weredog Lucy is priced based on the coloration selected. It costs $118.12 for the teak color or a solid custom color. A two color swirl costs $128.86 and the Signature and Hellhound colorations cost $144.96. The Weredog Lucy is somewhat expensive, but it is priced in the same range as other fantasy penetratable toys. The detailing and quality is excellent I think it is worth the price for anyone who likes the design.
