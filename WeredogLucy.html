Material & Quality

The Weredog Lucy is made with pure platinum cured silicone and body safe pigment for color. Weredog silicone comes in 3 firmness, all of their penetratables are made with their extra-soft blend. The silicone is soft and squishy but still holds its shape. It has a nice matte surface. Silicone is chemically stable and non-reactive, making it body safe. Because Silicone is non-porous it does not harbor bacteria making it easy to clean and disinfect. The Quality of the Weredog Lucy is exceptional. The colors are vibrant. Durability & Care

Platnum cured silicone is very durable, it does not tear easily or really wear from normal use. The toy does not have any small details that could be easily torn. The corners of the cookie opening are the only weak points I am concerned about, but they are reinforced and I think are unlikely to tear. Some silicone lubes can damage silicone toys making the surface sticky so the Lucy should only be used with water or oil based lubes.
The Lucy is easy to clean after use because it has double openings. I wash it out then allow it to dry before storing it in the included bag. Because it is pure silicone it can be sterilized by boiling, but I have not found it necessary to fully sterilize it.

Packaging & Price

The Lucy shipped in an discreet cardboard box. Inside the box the toy was packaged in a simple heat sealed plastic bag wrapped in a branded cotton storage bag. The packaging is similar to other small you brands I have purchased from in the past. Although the packaging is simple, I would much rather have a storage bag then a fancy box. It did take 20 days for the toy to arrive after ordering.
I purchased my Lucy during the Annual Weredog birthday sale on February 1st and received a 25% discount. The Weredog Lucy is priced by the coloration selected. It costs $118.12 for the teak color or a solid custom color. A two color swirl costs $128.86 and the Signature and Hellhound colorations cost $144.96. International Shipping cost an additional $30. The Weredog Lucy is somewhat expensive, but it is priced in the same range as other fantasy penetratable toys. The detailing and quality is excellent I think it is worth the price for anyone who likes the design.
