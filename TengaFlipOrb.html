Material & Quality

The hard exterier shell is made of polycarbonate which is durable and relatively lightweight. The inner sleve is made of Thermoplastic elastomer (TPE) which feels good but can harbor bacteria if improperly cleaned. The orbs are made out of ABS plastic and completely encased in the TPE sleeve. I have had the Tenga Flip Orb for a couple of years and it is starting to show signs of wear. Some of the nubs of the TPE sleeve have gotten worn or stretched out. I was initially concerned that the hinge would break, because it appears somewhat delicate, but it has withstood vigorous use for over a year.

The Flip orb was packaged in a simple clear plastic clamshell. The packaging has minimal decoration designed to highlight the toy and its features. The packaging serves its purpose of protecting the toy and can be disposed of discreetly. There were 2 sachets of Tenga lube. The flip orb costs $69 (nice!), which is a reasonable price for a reusable toy from a renowned sex toy manufacturer.
