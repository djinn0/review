Hot Octopus has since expanded their toy offerings producing innovative and inclusive toys. The Pulse Duo is based on the original Penis specific design of the pulse line of toys, but includes an extra motor and remote for use with a partner.

Packaging & Price

The Pulse Duo was packaged in a blue and black box with white detailing and text. The packaging is stylish, but the cardboard is thinner and the packaging feels cheaper than other toys in a similar price range I have tried. In the box was the vibrator, a charging chord, remote control, storage bag, and instruction manual. The Pulse Duo retails for $149.95 on the Hot Octopus website, which is comparably priced to similar toys like the Fun Factory Manta. I like how the Pulse Duo feels, but I do not think that the couples features add much. I wished I saved $50 and just got the Pulse Solo instead of the Duo.
