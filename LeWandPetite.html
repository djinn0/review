Introduction

The Le Wand petite is my wifes favorite toy, and definitely gets used more then anything else in the toy box. The Le Wand Petite is a rechargable wand style vibrator and massager based on the classic Hitachi design. Although we owned the Le Wand Petite before I started this blog I have never gotten around to actually reviewing it. This review may be a bit different from many reviews of the wand because it is from my perspective as a partner, rather then from the perspective of the primary user.

Design

The Le Wand is a wand style vibrator in a more compact size. It has a silicone coated vibrating head, flexible neck and tappered handle. It is approximately 10 inches in length and the head is 1.5 inchs in diameter. There are 3 buttons on the handle: a plus button which increases vibration intensity, a minus button which decreses vibration intensity, and a wave button that changes the vibration pattern. There is a port to insert a charging cord at the base of the handel. It currently comes in 5 colors: rose gold, dark cherry, blue, violet, and navy. We have the rose gold color.
