Fuze Pleasure Plug Review

Introduction

The Pleasure plug 3 is a lovely classically styled buttplug made by Fuze toys, a small Canadian sextoy manufacturer. There are almost no reviews for Fuze Pleasure Plugs which is a shame because it is one my favorite plugs, so I decided to write one. Fuze makes the Pleasure Plug in 4, sizes this review is for size 3 because I like it best.

Design

The Pleasure Plug 3 is medium sized with a classic design tapered design. The plug is 5 inches long, has an insertable length of 4.75 inches and a diameter of 1.4 inches. The plugs gentle taper from the tip makes the plug easy to insert despite its size. The plug also has a nice inverse taper from the bulb to the neck, which makes the plug pleasant to pull out, but still holds it in securely in place. The neck is wide enough to provide a gentle stretching sensation, but pliable enough that it is comfortable for extended wear. The T-shaped base is the most modern feature of the plug, and is a pleasing departure from the classic buttplug design. The base is sturdy enough that the plug can not be pulled in, but nestles comfortably and discreetly between the butt cheeks

Experience

First ImpressionWhen I purchased the the Fuze Pleasure Plug 3, it was the longest buttplug I had ever tried and it was a a little intimidating (I have tried a variety of plugs but I am still a novice when it comes to size). I warmed up with the Tantus Juice a shorter plug with the same diameter as the pleasure plug 3. I relaxed and took my time inserting the plug, which slid in quite easily after a bit of warm-up.

Material & Quality

The Pure plug is made from Fuzes premium silicone blend. It has a shiny and smooth surface, which gets very slick when lubed but also attracts lint. The silicone is pliable and comfortable but firm enough to be easy to insert. The Pleasure plug has a simple but well executed design and feels well made. The plug is a beautiful purple color. Although there is a seam from the molding process it can barely be seen and it cannot be felt. The silicon does not retain scent after being cleaned.

Durability & Care

Premium platinum cured silicone is quite durable. The Pleasure plug 3 feels like it will last for ever if care for. The plug does not have any crevices so it is easy to clean. After use I wash it with soap and hot water, then allow it to dry. I wipe the plug with 70% ethanol to sterilize the surface. The shiny silicone attracts lint so I store it in a microfiber sunglasses pouch.

Packaging & Price

The Fuze Pleasure Plug 3 came in a clear plastic box, with minimal ornamentation. The clear box displays the plugs size and design and highlights its lovely color. The box is durable enough to store the plug in or can be desposed of discreetly. The Pleasure plug costs $42.50 which is on the expensive side for a simple silicone plug.
