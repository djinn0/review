Introduction

The Sapphire is a fantasy inspired stroker made by Weredog Creations, a UK based indie toy manufacturer. Weredog was founded to produce fantasy adult products for the european market but have expanded and ship toys all over the world.

Design

The Sapphire is a dragon inspired stroker. It has dragon scale and spine detailing on the outside. It has a small entrance, a wider rippled chamber which narrows into the main channel

Material & Quality

The Sapphire is made of premium hand poured silicone. Weredog uses a super soft silicone blend for all penetratables. The Sapphire is excellent quality The detailing is intricate and the coloring is vibrant and precise.

Durability & Care

Weredog states that their silicon "Its's strong and flexible, so it will take the roughage of average use without any worry if splits or tears. With that said it will cut if you take a sharp implement to it

Packaging & Price

The Weredog Sapphire was packaged in a opaque polyurethane heat sealed bag with Weredog branding. The packaging was similar to other small manufacturers that I have purchased from in the past. I like simple bag for packaging because it keeps the toy clean in transit, produces minimal waste, and is easy to dispose of discreetly. Weredog included a branded storage sack to store the toy which is a nice touch. The Sapphire is priced based on selected colors with a base price of $115.84. I got the signature style which fades from light blue to dark blue with white accents which costs $142.16. The Sapphire is similarly priced to other premium silicone fantasy toys; It's somewhat expensive, but it is a reasonable investment in pleasure.
