Packaging & Price

The Weredog Sapphire was packaged in a opaque polyurethane heat sealed bag with Weredog branding. The packaging was similar to other small manufacturers that I have purchased from in the past. I like simple bag for packaging because it keeps the toy clean in transit, produces minimal waste, and is easy to dispose of discreetly. Weredog included a branded storage sack to store the toy which is a nice touch. The Sapphire is priced based on selected colors with a base price of $115.84. I got the signature style which fades from light blue to dark blue with white accents which costs $142.16.
