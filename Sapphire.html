Introduction

The Sapphire is a fantasy inspired stroker made by Weredog Creations, a UK based indie toy manufacturer. Weredog was founded to produce fantasy adult products for the european market but have expanded and ship toys all over the world.

Design

The Sapphire is a dragon inspired stroker. It is an open ended design, made with soft platinum-cured silicone. The Sapphire has dragon scale and spine detailing on the outside. It has a small entrance, a wider rippled chamber which narrows into the main channel. It comes in 2 sizes narrow and wide, this review is for the wide. I usually do not like anatomical styled toys, they look like severed appendages which I find off-putting, so I usually choose toys with abstract functional designs. The Sapphire is the first fantasy toy I have tried and for me the styling improved the experience, so I will likely test more fantasy toys in the future.

Material & Quality

The Sapphire is made of premium platinum-cured silicone. Weredog uses a super soft silicone blend for all penetratables. The silicone has a smooth somewhat sticky surface, it gets very slick when wet and unfortunately attracts lint when dry. The Sapphire is great quality, The exterrier detailing is intricate and the coloring is vibrant and precise.

Durability & Care

Weredog states the following about the durability of their silicon: "Its's strong and flexible, so it will take the roughage of average use without any worry of splits or tears. With that said, it will cut if you take a sharp implement to it!" In my experience the Sapphire appears to be well made, there are no thin ridges or delicate details that can be torn off

The Weredog Sapphire was packaged in a opaque polyurethane heat sealed bag with Weredog branding. The packaging was similar to other small manufacturers that I have purchased from in the past. I like when a simple bag is used as packaging because it keeps the toy clean in transit, produces minimal waste, and is easy to dispose of discreetly. Weredog included a branded storage sack to store the toy which is a nice touch. The Sapphire is priced based on the selected color with a base price of $115.84. I got the signature style which fades from light blue to dark blue with white accents and costs $142.16. The Sapphire is similarly priced to other premium silicone fantasy toys. It's somewhat expensive, but it is a reasonable investment in pleasure.
