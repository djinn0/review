Durability & Care

The Vice has a IPX7 water resistance rating, meaning it can be submerged for 30 min at a depth of up to 1 meter without suffering damage. The Vice 2 can be cleaned with hot water and soap. The silicone surface can be sanitized with alcohol. The remote is not water resistant and should not be washed, it can be cleaned by wiping it with a damp cloth. I store the Vice 2 in a microfiber pouch between uses which keeps it free from lint and allows scents to dissipate.
