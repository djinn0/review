The Liberator Jazz motion is made with high density polyurethane foam which is soft enough to be comfortable but much firmer and supportive than a normal pillow. The Jazz has a microsuede cover that is soft to the touch and machine washable. The cover has a water-resistant liner that protects the pillow from moisture and makes clean-up easy. The cover has a beefy zipper and us thick and feels very durable. The Liberator feels well made, and is as good quality as I expected.
