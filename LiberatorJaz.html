Introduction

Liberator is a US based manufacture of furnature and intimate accessories. Liberator is best known for their positioning wedges but also make sex furniture, harnesses, waterproof blankets, sex toys and other products. I have wanted to try a wedge for a while, but they are expensive and were always other toys I wanted to test more. When the Jaz Motion went in sale I decided it was time to buy a positioning pillow.

Design

The Jaz Motion is one of the smaller wedges Liberator makes. It is 19 inches in length, 12 inches in width and 6 inches thick at the widest point. The angle of the wedge is approximately 20 degrees. The Jazz motion has a curved side and a flat side. It can be used as a simple wedge with flat side down or it can enhance rocking motion when used with the curved side down. It has a simple but effective design.

Experience

My wife and I tested the Liberator Jaz Motion in multiple positions. It improved some positions enhancing stimulation and making them more comfortable.

Material & Quality

The Liberator Jazz motion is made with high density polyurethane foam which is soft enough to be comfortable but much firmer and supportive than a normal pillow. The Jazz has a microsuede cover that is soft to the touch and machine washable. The cover has a water-resistant liner that protects the pillow from moisture and makes clean-up easy. The cover has a beefy zipper and us thick and feels very durable. The Liberator feels well made, and is as good quality as I expected.

Durability & Care

The Polyurethane foam that is at the core of the Liberator Jazz Motion is extremely Durable. Polyurethane foam is long lasting, resists moisture, withstands mechanical stress and was even used to insulate the fuel tank of the space shuttle. The cover also seems quite sturdy with thick fabric and a strong zipper. The pillow usually stays pretty clean, but I am glad that the cover is machine washable if things get a bit messy.

Packaging & Price

The Jaz Motion costs $120 on the Liberator website. During Liberators memorial day sale it was on sale for half off making it a reasonable $60. The pillow was shipped in a discrete brown cardboard box. The inside of the box was white with liberator branding in red, stickers, a post card and a small catalog The Jaz was vacuumed packed to conserve space. I think the box with a plain exterier and stylish interior is a clever way to reduce shipping materials and environmental impact while still providing an exciting experience when the box is opened.
